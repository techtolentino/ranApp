The code less traveled

The first thing you should know is that I'm truly blessed and lucky to be where I am today. To put it simply - I have a beautiful family that I get to experience life with, every day. I also have an amazing career as a Software Product Design Professional at IBM. And, there is a certain type of momentum I see in myself that I have never seen before.

The truth be told, it hasn't always been this way. In fact, we once lived in a small, crammed bedroom in a relative's house in San Diego, California. I was working for the YMCA, making about $15 an hour, and expected to support a family of four. It sucked.
